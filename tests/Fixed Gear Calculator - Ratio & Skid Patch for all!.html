Ratioclose

The ratio of chainring teeth to rear sprocket teeth, in other words, how many times your rear wheel turns with each revolution of the pedals.

For example :

- ratio < 1,9 : Bike polo
- 1,9 < ratio < 2,3 : lot of steep slopes
- 2,3 < ratio < 2,7 : polyvalent ratio
- ratio > 2,7 : high speed on flat roads (take care of your knees)
